FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first lesson.

What age can my child start lessons? +

We accept children as young as 3 for skiing and 5 for snowboarding. Our kids programs are designed with age-appropriate techniques and lots of fun activities!

Do you provide equipment? +

Equipment is not included but can be rented at any of our partner resort locations. We can help you find the right gear for your lesson.

What's your cancellation policy? +

Full refund with 48+ hours notice. 50% refund with 24-48 hours notice. Weather cancellations are always fully refunded or rescheduled.

Where do lessons take place? +

We operate at Blue Mountain, Mount St. Louis Moonstone, and Horseshoe Resort. You choose the location that's most convenient for you.

How do I know my skill level? +

We offer a free skill assessment before your first group lesson. For private lessons, your instructor will assess and adapt on the first day.

What should we wear/bring? +

Layered clothing, waterproof jacket and pants, warm gloves, helmet (required for kids), and goggles. We'll send a complete checklist when you book.
